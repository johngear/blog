Large Language Models (LLMs) refer to entites like ChatGPT, Bing's Sydney or Google's Lamda. These are massive transformer-based machine learning models, trained on mind-numbingly large sets of text, and tuned with human feedback. They end up being pretty good at writing and reading as a result.

One thing LLMs have been documented at excelling at is, when prompted, giving convincing statements about their "conciousness". This has many people concerned.
